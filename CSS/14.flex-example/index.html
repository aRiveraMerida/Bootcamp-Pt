Marvel Reveals Its 2023 Free Comic Book Day Titles

For next year’s FREE COMIC BOOK DAY, Marvel Comics will have something for every fan, with four separate titles packed with exciting entry points into the Marvel Comics mythos! In addition to lead-in stories to the biggest comic book sagas of 2023, including FALL OF X and SUMMER OF SYMBIOTES, 2023’s FREE COMIC BOOK DAY titles will also spotlight creators and characters from different cultures, communities, and identities and provide the perfect first comic for new readers!
